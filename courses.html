Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our robotics courses for kids

What is a robotic course for kids?

A robotics course for kids is a fun, interactive program where children learn to design, build, and program robots. These courses teach problem-solving, coding, and engineering skills, while encouraging creativity and teamwork.

What age group is suitable for a robotic course?

We offer courses for children aged 6 to 20, with programs tailored to different age groups and skill levels. Each course is designed to be age-appropriate, ensuring that content is engaging and challenging without being overwhelming.

Do kids need prior knowledge of robotics or coding?

No prior experience is needed for our beginner courses. We start with fundamental concepts and gradually build skills. For more advanced courses, we recommend completing the prerequisite levels or having equivalent experience.

What will kids learn in a robotic course?

Students learn programming, mechanical design, problem-solving, and teamwork. They'll work with sensors, motors, and controllers to build functioning robots while developing logical thinking and creativity.
