Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our robotics programs for kids

What is a robotic program for kids?

A robotics program for kids is a structured learning experience where children engage in designing, building, and programming robots. These programs teach problem-solving, coding, and engineering skills while encouraging creativity and teamwork through various formats like classes, camps, and competitions.

What age group is suitable for your programs?

We offer programs for children aged 6 to 18, with different programs tailored to specific age groups and skill levels. Each program is designed to be age-appropriate, ensuring that content is engaging and challenging without being overwhelming.

Do kids need prior knowledge of robotics or coding?

No prior experience is needed for our beginner programs. We start with fundamental concepts and gradually build skills. For more advanced programs, we recommend completing our beginner levels or having equivalent experience.

What will kids learn in your programs?

Students learn programming, mechanical design, problem-solving, and teamwork. They'll work with sensors, motors, and controllers to build functioning robots while developing logical thinking and creativity. Specific skills vary by program type and level.
